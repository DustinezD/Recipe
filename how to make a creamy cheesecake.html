♥The Creamiest Cheesecake♥

Ingredients

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • Sugar (3 tbsp)
  • Butter (1-2 Pc/s)
  • Cinnamon (1-2 Pc/s)
  • Cream cheese (0.5 kg)
  • Heavy cream (4 cloves)
  • Fresh eggs (1 Pc)
  • Vanilla (1 pc)
  • Lemon Juice (1 tbsp)
  • Rasberry (1 cup)
  • Water (1.5 cups)
  • Salt (0.25 tsp)
  • Graham (1 box)

Steps to make The Creamiest Cheesecake Ever!

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Sauté potato and carrot in 1 tbsp oil until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  2. Season chicken with Afritada Sauce. Sauté chicken in oil, remove and set aside.
  3. Sauté garlic, onion and bell peppers. Pour tomato sauce and water. Add chicken, potato, carrot and bay leaf. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Season with remaining sachet of your preferred Chicken Afritada Sauce, pepper and sugar. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
